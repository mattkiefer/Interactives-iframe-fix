A look into Super Bowl LIX

Super Bowl LIX is scheduled for Sunday, Feb. 9, at the Caesars Superdome in New Orleans, LA, with Taylor Swift's boyfriend Travis Kelce playing for the Kansas City Chiefs against the Philadephia Eagles.

There's a lot of buzz in the media in anticipation, from Kendrick Lamar's Halftime Show to celebrity sightings in the famous Super Bowl ads.

Kendrick Lamar just won the Record and Song of the Year at the Grammys for "Not Like Us," which he will also be performing at the Super Bowl Halftime Show. Apple Music revealed that he will be headlining with guest artist SZA.
